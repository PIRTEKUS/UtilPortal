PIRTEK USA Hydraulic
and Industrial Hose Service

PIRTEK USA provides the fastest hydraulic and industrial hose maintenance and replacement services through on-site mobile and retail PIRTEK Service & Supply centers throughout the United States. Our 150+ locations offer a 1-Hour ETA for on-site emergency hose service, available 24/7/365, and provide a simple and quick solution that minimizes equipment downtime and eliminates the need for customers to leave their job site or facility to locate new hydraulic or industrial hoses and fittings.

PIRTEK Signature Products

PIRTEK franchise locations carry a large selection of hydraulic and industrial hoses with a wide assortment of fittings needed to fabricate hose assemblies on demand. The PIRTEK range of products is designed to meet the diverse requirements of customers of all sizes and from a broad cross-section of industries.

Hydraulic Hoses

PIRTEK meets all your hydraulic hose needs with medium to high to ultra-high-pressure hoses.

Industrial Hoses

From aggregate to chemical to air hoses and more, PIRTEK has all the industrial hoses to support a diverse range of industries.

Adapters

PIRTEK offers one of the most extensive catalogs of adapters to fit almost any hose replacement job.
